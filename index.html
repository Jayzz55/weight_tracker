Welcome to WEIGHT TRACKER

We want to present you a simple and and easy to use weight tracker tool that keeps track of your weight daily like a journal. You can set a goal and check your current BMI. WEIGHT TRACKER also show you the history data that you can visually see your progress and help you manage your weight more effectively.
